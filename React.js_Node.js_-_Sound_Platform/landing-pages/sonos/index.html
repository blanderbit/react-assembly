Play now Soundsuit® on Sonos®

Soundsuit is a certified Sonos partner. To stream music from Soundsuit on your Sonos® speaker(s):

  1. Register for Soundsuit’s 30-day free trial.
  2. In the Sonos® controller, click “Add Music Services” and select Soundsuit from the list.
  3. Log in with your Soundsuit® login details. Enjoy!
