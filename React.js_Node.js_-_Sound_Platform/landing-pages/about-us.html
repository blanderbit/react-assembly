About us

Soundsuit is an online music service for point-of-sale.

Our mission:

Soundsuit makes tailored music for businesses the norm rather then a luxury.

Our motto:

Play Better Music.

Our slogan:

Soundsuit is the easiest way for stores to sound great to their customers.

1. Short history

Soundsuit - as a company - was officially born in November 2013, few weeks after that Soundsuit's concept was primed at the Bavarian Startup Awards.

The product was designed and developed over one year, together with potential customers at the heart of the quarter of Haidhausen, in Munich.

Renown for its density and quality of venues, cafés, restaurants, fashion and interior design stores, it was the ideal place for Soundsuit's team to test their idea and mature their concept. Face to face with small business owners, learning from them and adjusting to meet their needs.

From this participative experience was born the Soundsuit's app as you know it today. Easy-to-use, beautiful and intelligent.
